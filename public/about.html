About

What does our website do?

Have you ever wanted to donate your old stuff to charities but found it too troublesome? Our website can help you find the nearest charity who needs your stuff the most. You just need to provide us with your location and the type of goods that you would like to donate.

Furthermore, you are more than welcomed to check the special events held by various charities on our website! Your kindness will be appreciated.

What if I am a charity?

Feel free to use our website to find more donation that fits your needs! Just fill out the application form and become one of our members. Besides, you can always set up special events.
